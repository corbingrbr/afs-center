General

  • Our building is approximately 13,000 ft2, with capability to expand to 20,000 ft2
  • 2500 ft2 of raised floor environment which can support up to 90 48" cabinets at 6 kW/cabinet
  • Tier-4 generators, most up to date Liebert NXL UPS system, and custom built AC units with free cooling mode

Security

The security system in use is the S2 Security System, which provides the following:

  • 24/7 monitoring
  • No limitations on device capabilities, including but not limited to, hand scanners, retinal detectors, proximity badge readers, fixed/PTZ cameras
  • Data reporting of all the aforementioned systems
  • Customizable levels of access control, rannging from office access to critical facility biometric access.

More information about the S2 Security System can be found at s2sys.com

Fire Protection

  • State of the art fire protection
  • Double-interlock preaction sprinkler system in all critical areas, including raised floor and UPS rooms
  • Non-critical areas are protected with a wet system
  • Cross-zoned smoke detectors provide detection throughout the facility
  • The FACP is manufactured by Notifier

Building Management

  • Building management system and automation operated by Opto 22 hardware and controls
  • Opto 22 products run on non-proprietary communication standards, such as Ethernet and Internet Protocol (IP)
  • High precision system control at lower operational costs
  • Access to five major fiber optic service providers

Power

  • Utility power supplied by Salt River Project (SRP)
  • Non-dedicated feeds from two separate sub stations
  • Two Emerson Network Power NXL UPS modules, each rated at 400kVA/360kW for dual path redundancy to critical loads
  • Four tier-4 Caterpillar Engine Generators which cooperate to offer N+1 redundancy
  • Ability to expand critical system capacities to match future client needs

Cooling

  • Custom roof top AC units built with 100% outside air economizers providing free cooling, weather permitting
  • Cross zone cooling configuration
  • AC provided to the raised floor environment is done through the ceiling, allowing for use of underfloor without consideration for air flow
